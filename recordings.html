📚 Qirāʾāt — Seven Modes of Recitation

The Sabʿah Qirāʾāt are the seven canonical modes of Qurʾānic recitation, each transmitted through an unbroken chain back to the Prophet ﷺ. Qāriʾ Nazrul is a teacher of the Sabʿah and Thalāthah Qirāʾāt.
